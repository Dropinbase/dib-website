T&C's Overview

  • Dropinbase is free for internal use by employees of startups with less than $30,000 (USD) annual revenue.
  • Dropinbase is free for internal use by employees/workers of registered non-profits.
  • Dropinbase is free for personal use and for learning to use Dropinbase.
  • Pay one subscription fee per application, covering any number of development, testing or staging installations, but only one production installation.
  • Self-hosted: unlimitted users, databases and tables.
  • Secondary production installations of the same application are subject to their own subscription fee.
  • Subscription fees are due after the first two months, irrespective of whether an application is used in production yet, unless it is 'discontinued'.
  • All tiers have access to all core features of Dropinbase.
  • Dropinbase may not be used for unlawful or unethical purposes. Certain activities are strictly prohibited.
  • If Dropinbase is forked/changed, it must be for internal use only and not be made public. Subscription fees are still payable.
  • Dropinbase is licensed, not sold. No part of Dropinbase (other than Permissable Parts) may be used in other products without our written permission.
  • Subscription fees may be adjusted from time to time - we will strive to keep them low.
  • Due to the low-cost, subscription fees are non-refundable and non-transferable.
